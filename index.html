How to Flash Firmware

1

Download Image

Download your chosen firmware image and extract the ZIP file to a USB stick.

2

Prepare USB

Format a USB stick as FAT32 and copy the extracted files to the root directory.

3

Flash Receiver

Insert USB into your ZGemma 4 while powered off, then hold the reset button and power on.

4

Configure

Follow the on-screen setup wizard to scan for channels and configure your preferences.
