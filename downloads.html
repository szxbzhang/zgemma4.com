Important Notice
ZGEMMA supplies all receivers strictly as Free-to-Air (FTA) hardware only. We do not provide receivers pre-loaded with any patches and cannot assist with any non-FTA usage. All firmware images below are provided by independent open-source community teams.
Official Image Providers
Enigma2 Image Partners
OpenATV
The most widely used Enigma2 image. Stable, well-maintained, and highly recommended for all ZGEMMA devices. Updated regularly with new features and hardware support.
Visit OpenATV →
OpenPLi
Community favourite image with a focus on stability and performance. Large user base with extensive plugin support. Ideal for IPTV and streaming setups.
Visit OpenPLi →
OpenViX
Feature-rich Enigma2 image with an active development team. Excellent plugin ecosystem and regular updates for all supported ZGEMMA models.
Visit OpenViX →
OpenBH (BlackHole)
Stable BlackHole-based Enigma2 image. Known for reliability and clean interface. Supported across a wide range of ZGEMMA hardware models.
Visit OpenBH →
OpenSPA
Spanish-developed Enigma2 image with strong European community backing. Feature-complete with good multi-language support.
Visit OpenSPA →
Pure-E2
Lightweight Enigma2 image focused on performance. Minimal footprint with all essential features. Good choice for experienced users.
Visit Pure-E2 →
