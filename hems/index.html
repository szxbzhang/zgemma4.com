Capabilities
What HEMS Does
📊

Real-time Dashboard

Live energy flow visualization on your TV — solar generation, battery state, grid import/export and household consumption updated every few seconds.

🔌

Multi-brand Support

Over 30 brand drivers included. Mix and match solar inverters, batteries, smart plugs and meters from different manufacturers in one unified view.

⚡

Smart Automation

Create rules to automate devices based on energy conditions. Turn on a smart plug when solar excess exceeds 1 kW, or start your dishwasher at peak production.

🔋

Battery Management

Track state of charge, charge/discharge rates and health metrics for Tesla Powerwall, BYD, Pylontech, Sonnen and other home battery systems.

🚗

EV Smart Charging

Solar-surplus EV charging integration with go-e, OpenEVSE and Wallbox chargers. Charge your car with free solar energy automatically.

🌡️

Heat Pump Control

Monitor and optimize heat pump operation with Daikin and MELCloud (Mitsubishi) integration. Schedule heating around cheap energy or solar surplus.

Setup
Three Steps to Energy Insight
01

Install the Plugin

Transfer the .ipk file to your ZGEMMA box and install via the Enigma2 package manager or command line.

02

Configure Your Devices

Edit devices.json to add your inverter, battery, smart plugs and other devices with their local IP addresses.

03

View on Your TV

Launch HEMS from the Enigma2 plugin menu and see your complete energy flow dashboard in real time on your TV screen.
